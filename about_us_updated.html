Our Mission

To empower Bangladeshi homes and businesses with sustainable, affordable, and locally-tailored smart automation solutions using recycled materials, modular designs, and Bangla-friendly interfaces.

Our History

Bytebots was established by a team of proficient engineering students from the Departments of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering and Chemical Engineering at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). Leveraging their academic expertise in embedded systems, sustainable design, and materials engineering, the founders aimed to address the pressing needs of Bangladeshi households and institutions.
